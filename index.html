About Me

Creating human-centered digital experiences

I'm a product designer who bridges creativity and functionality. I specialize in UI/UX design, visual systems, and translating complex user needs into intuitive, accessible interfaces.

With a Postgraduate Diploma in Web and Mobile App Design from Langara College, I bring both design thinking and technical understanding to every project.
