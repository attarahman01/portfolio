About Me

UI/UX Designer based in Vancouver

I'm a Mechanical Engineer turned UI/UX Designer who bridges structural logic with human-centered design. I specialize in UI/UX design, visual systems, and translating complex user needs into intuitive, accessible interfaces.

With a Postgraduate Diploma in Web and Mobile App Design from Langara College, I bring both design thinking and technical understanding to every project.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me gaming, sketching, or exploring Vancouver's creative scene.
