Rahmani

A luxury wine branding project focused on heritage, elegance, and premium tactile packaging for the modern connoisseur.

Industry Wine & Spirits
Client Rahmani Vineyards
Role Lead Designer
Duration 6 Weeks

Overview

As a Brand Designer, I set out to conceptually redefine non-alcoholic wine by embracing sophisticated, luxury aesthetics. The goal was to create a visual identity that speaks to mindful drinkers who desire the ritual of celebration without the alcohol.

This project encompasses brand strategy, logo refinement, and high-fidelity packaging design translating complex flavor profiles into elegant visual motifs.

The Concept

The core concept was to build a brand world around the idea of "The Ritual" — the act of pausing, appreciating, and connecting. Every design decision, from typeface to label texture, serves this narrative.

Brand Strategy

Redefining the non-alcoholic segment required a strategy that moves away from "diet" alternatives and towards a standalone luxury experience.

Market Position

Positioning Rahmani as a premium lifestyle choice for the "mindful epicurean"—those who value high-quality ingredients and aesthetic ritual above all else.

Visual Language

Utilizing dark, moody palettes and intricate botanical etchings to contrast with the minimalist, bright designs typically found in the non-alcoholic market.

Design Process

01. Research

Deep dive into classic wine label architecture and historical botanical illustrations.

02. Ideation

Iterating on logo-marks that balance modern slab-serifs with fluid, organic forms.

03. Execution

Translating brand assets into high-fidelity tactile packaging and digital rollouts.

Branding & Typography

Crimson #BD202E
Alabaster #F4F4F4
Obsidian #231F20

Parfumeire

Serving as the primary display typography. Its elegant, high-contrast structure beautifully evokes the sophistication and luxury of premium wine labels.

Futura

Used for secondary headings and structural accents. Its timeless geometric precision offers a sharp, modern contrast to the organic display font.

Satoshi

Employed for body copy and detailed text. Satoshi provides exceptional readability, balancing the expressive luxury fonts with refined, neutral clarity.

Brand & Packaging

A curated selection of high-fidelity mockups showcasing the Rahmani brand across various luxury mediums and tactile packaging.
