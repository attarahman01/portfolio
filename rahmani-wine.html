Rahmani

A premium non-alcoholic wine redefining celebration with elegance and sophisticated flavor profiles, designed for the modern mindful drinker.

Industry Food & Beverage
Client Rahmani
Role Brand Designer
Duration 4 Weeks

Overview

As a Brand Designer, I set out to conceptually redefine non-alcoholic wine by embracing sophisticated, luxury aesthetics. The goal was to create a visual identity that speaks to mindful drinkers who desire the ritual of celebration without the alcohol.

This project encompasses brand strategy, logo refinement, and high-fidelity packaging designâ€”translating complex flavor profiles into elegant visual motifs.

The Concept

The visual narrative of Rahmani centers on elegance and nature. Rather than typical, overly minimal designs common in the non-alcoholic space, I opted for intricate botanical illustrations combined with striking, premium packaging. The brand voice is moody, intimate, and designed to foster meaningful, atmospheric connections.

Color Choice

Deep Purple

Evokes the richness of wine and twilight elegance. The anchor of the brand.

Warm Cream

Provides softness, grounding the high-contrast luxury with an organic touch.
