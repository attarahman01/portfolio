VEURA

Luxury scent branding built for mood, emotion, and sophisticated atmosphere.

View Figma File
Industry Luxury Goods
Client Concept Brand
Role Visual Designer
Duration 4 Weeks

MY ROLE

As the Lead Visual Designer, I defined the brand strategy and digital identity, focusing on translating olfactory concepts into a cohesive visual experience.
My work encompassed color theory, typography selection, and high-fidelity UI design, moving beyond functional UX to establish a premium, mood-driven brand atmosphere.

THE PROBLEM

Fragrance brands often rely on overly traditional or minimal web designs that donâ€™t capture the scent, story, or emotion behind their products. Many lack a strong brand voice or visual flow that keeps users engaged past the first scroll.

SOLUTION

A digital experience that evokes scent through visual storytelling.

Emotive Design

Use color and texture to evoke scent digitally, creating a sensory connection.

Storytelling Flow

Guide users through an immersive brand narrative that unfolds like a fragrance.

Modern Luxury

Blend high-end aesthetics with accessible interaction for a premium feel.

DESIGN PROCESS

01

Concept Art

Explored visual metaphors for scent using macro photography and abstract textures to build a unique brand mood.

02

Color Theory

Developed a palette that balances traditional luxury (Gold/Black) with modern softness (Cream).

03

Layout Design

Designed a homepage that prioritizes large-scale imagery and smooth scrolling animations.

04

Refinement

Polished interactions and typography to ensure every detail reinforced the premium brand.

BRANDING & TYPOGRAPHY

A visual identity designed to feel invisible yet elegant.

  • Gold: Represents luxury and warmth.
  • Black: Provides sophistication and contrast.
  • Cream: Adds softness and modern appeal.

SUMMARY

VEURA stands as a testament to the power of emotive storytelling in the luxury sector. By blending high-end aesthetics with modern digital interactions, we created a brand experience that feels as sophisticated as the scents themselves. The project resulted in a cohesive visual language that scales across all platforms.
