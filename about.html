About Me

Designing clear, usable digital experiences for real people

I’m Atta Rahman, a product designer focused on clarity, usability, and thoughtful interaction. I enjoy breaking down complex problems and shaping them into interfaces that feel intuitive, intentional, and easy to use.

My Approach

I believe good design should feel natural and effortless.

My approach starts with understanding people, their needs, and the context around a problem before moving into structure and visuals.

With a postgraduate diploma in Web and Mobile App Design from Langara College, I combine design thinking with hands-on technical knowledge to build practical, well-considered digital experiences.

My background in engineering and operations influences how I work. I value structure, attention to detail, and designing systems that hold up beyond a single screen.

Outside of design, I enjoy gaming, sketching ideas, and exploring Vancouver’s creative spaces for inspiration.

Based In Vancouver, BC

How I Work

My design process is iterative, collaborative, and focused on delivering measurable results.

01

Understand

I start by deeply understanding the problem, users, and business goals through research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis.

02

Explore

I translate insights into user flows, wireframes, and early concepts—focusing on structure, hierarchy, and solving the core problem.

03

Iterate

Through feedback and testing, I refine designs to improve usability, accessibility, and visual consistency—always keeping users at the center.

04

Deliver

I deliver polished, developer-ready designs with detailed specs, ensuring seamless handoff and pixel-perfect implementation.
