Receiptly

Receiptly is an AI powered tool that automates expense tracking, helping startups save time and reduce errors by turning financial chaos into clear insights.

View Figma file View Proposal
Industry FinTech
Client Code & Canvas
Role Product Design
Duration 13 Weeks

My Role

As lead Product Designer, I was responsible for the end-to-end design process, from user research and wireframing to the final visual interface.

I focused on creating a dashboard that balances automation with transparency, ensuring users trust the AI while gaining immediate financial clarity.

The Problem

Startups and small businesses waste valuable time manually sorting receipts, tracking expenses, and preparing financial reports. This manual process increases the risk of errors and takes focus away from strategic growth.

01.

Founders are overwhelmed by scattered financial data and lack real-time visibility.

02.

Manual entry errors lead to inaccurate reporting and compliance risks.

Solution

Receiptly automates receipt capture and categorization using AI. It offers a simple, intuitive experience designed specifically for small teams.

Smart Capture

AI instantly extracts data from receipts, eliminating manual entry.

Real-time Insights

Founders get immediate visibility into cash flow and spending categories.

Investor-Ready

Generate clean, professional financial reports with one click.

User Personas

To understand the needs of our users, we created two distinct personas that represent the typical users of Receiptly.

Design Process

01. Research

Analyzed current workflows and pain points in manual data entry for small teams.

02. Wireframing

Iterated on dashboard layouts to prioritize quick access to financial summaries.

03. Visual Design

Developed a clean, professional visual language using blue and emerald to inspire trust.

Branding & Typography

Summary

Receiptly streamlines expense management for small teams, turning financial complexity into clarity. Automation reduces manual errors while maintaining transparency. The dashboard delivers quick insights at a glance, empowering founders to make confident decisions.
