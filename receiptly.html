Receiptly

Receiptly is an AI powered tool that automates expense tracking, helping startups save time and reduce errors by turning financial chaos into clear insights.

View Figma file View Proposal
Industry FinTech
Client Code & Canvas
Role Product Design
Duration 13 Weeks

My Role

As lead Product Designer, I was responsible for the end-to-end design process, from user research and wireframing to the final visual interface.

I focused on creating a dashboard that balances automation with transparency, ensuring users trust the AI while gaining immediate financial clarity.

Marketing Analysis

Tracking expenses is a critical but often painful process for startups and small teams, where manual entry and lack of clear structure can lead to significant financial oversights.

The Challenge

Many small business owners struggle with spreadsheets and complex accounting tools, often losing receipts or making mistakes during tax season.

The Receiptly Difference

Receiptly addresses this by providing a simple, fast, and flexible tool that organizes receipts, categorizes expenses, and gives users confidence that their finances are under control.

The Problem

Startups and small businesses waste valuable time manually sorting receipts, tracking expenses, and preparing financial reports. This manual process increases the risk of errors and takes focus away from strategic growth.

01. Scattered Data

Founders are overwhelmed by scattered financial data and lack real-time visibility.

02. Manual Errors

Manual entry errors lead to inaccurate reporting and compliance risks.

The Solution

We started by understanding how small business owners manage expenses, combining user interviews, competitor research, and analysis of tools like Zoho and Expensify. We found that most existing solutions were either too complex or costly for our audience.

From there, we designed AI-powered workflows for scanning receipts, categorizing expenses, and generating reports that are intuitive and reduce friction. Real-time updates allow users to see their finances clearly, turning raw numbers into actionable insights.

The focus was not just digitizing receipts, but creating a simple, reliable system designed specifically for small teams to automate the heavy lifting, helping users feel in control and confident about their finances.

Smart Capture

AI instantly extracts data from receipts, eliminating manual entry and reducing human error in bookkeeping flows.

Real-time Insights

Founders get immediate visibility into cash flow and spending categories without waiting for end-of-month reconciliations.

Investor-Ready

Generate clean, professional financial reports with one click. Always prepared for board meetings and tax seasons.

User Personas

To understand the needs of our users, we created two distinct personas that represent the typical users of Receiptly.

Design Process

01. Research

Analyzed current workflows and pain points in manual data entry for small teams.

02. Wireframing

Iterated on dashboard layouts to prioritize quick access to financial summaries.

03. Visual Design

Developed a clean, professional visual language using blue and emerald to inspire trust.

Branding & Typography

Primary Blue #2E39E6
Success Emerald #10B981
Background Light #FFFFFF
Text Dark #111827

Metro Sans

Chosen for modern readability, Metro Sans ensures that every component is clear and approachable. It supports efficiency and builds user confidence, which is vital for workflows.

Visual Language

We developed a comprehensive UI kit in Figma to establish a calming and trustworthy interface. The primary deep blue conveys reliability, while the soft light blue adds warmth.

Outcome & Learnings

Receiptly streamlines expense management for small teams, turning financial complexity into clarity. Automation reduces manual errors while maintaining transparency. The dashboard delivers quick insights at a glance, empowering founders to make confident decisions.
